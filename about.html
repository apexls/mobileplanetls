Get to know us

MANAGEMENT: Mobile Planet LS was founded and is under the management and supervision of MOKOTJO DOMINIC RAMOTHOBI. He is also the sole founder(picture displayed on the right). He holds a degree in soil science, obtained from the National University Lesotho (NUL). He is a young aspiring businessman,a logistics company owner and business manager.

ABOUT US: We are registered electronics on line store(registered with department of small business and LRA). We sell all kinds of Computers, gadgets(earphones and headphones, duplicate chargers and batteries, USB cables), accessories( mobile cases, flip covers, strings, screen guards), watches and mobile Phones, but Mainly Apple Products, We operate 24hours.

HOW WE OPERATE: We dont have a physical store yet. We operate online. The way we operate depends on the availability of the product a customer is purchasing. If a product is readily available, we use CASH ON DELIVERY( COD). If the required product is out of the stock, a customer places an order by paying 70% deposit upfront. Deliveries are made every FRIDAY, SATURDAY AND SUNDAY. We deliver Local, but outside Maseru district we charge a deliver fee depending on Customers Location.

OUR VISION: Our Vision is to grow our business to the extent where we supply telecommunications companies in Lesotho, “Vodacom Lesotho and Econet Lesotho” and outlet stores that sells electronic devices. Apart from that, to be consistently selling abroad. To Build trust through accountability, loyalty, fairness and integrity, also to Communicate clearly, openly and on a timely basis with our Customers.
